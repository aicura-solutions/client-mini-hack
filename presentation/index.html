# Open Source notes: - I believe open source is for everybody - Yes you! - It is not just for techies - It doesnt care what you do, what your job title is, how much you earn, what you are wearing - The only thing open source cares about is you pay it a visit, few hours, or few weeks
## Why Open Source Transparency, Visibility, Collaboration, Authenticity notes: - People want to be part of something bigger than themselves - Higher quality, not just features and less bugs but including security, performance, accessibility - Maintainers + Contributors are both proud of their work - friends, colleagues, future employers can see - Great way to hire people - they know your team, your project
## What is Open Source Social coding notes: - GitHub the main platform for open source strapline is “social coding” - Facebook / Instragram for code - Now it has recipes, blogs, templates, books, science, education, legal documents etc - open hardware designs including robots, cars, houses, bridges etc
## Who Google, Microsoft, Netflix, Facebook, Twitter, Adobe, Guardian, Uber, Amazon, Monzo, Starling Bank, Revolut, FreeAgent, Governments notes: - Lloyds have just started, sent their team to GitHub Universe - Their 2019 mission as a group is open source first - Collaborating with the challenger banks (already with Monzo) - Lloyds have realised they dont need faster horses, they needs cars
## Example projects Angular, React, Bootstrap, Material, NodeJS notes: - Some of these haver over 30k contributors
## Projects 96M+ repos, 200M+ contributions notes: - Vscode 20k contributors - Angular 10k contributors - People hours: 20k -> 10k -> 1hour a month -> 5k x 1hr = over 625 working days = team of over 30 - Trends: javascript is the most popular language
## People 31M+ people, 8M+ new people, 2.1M+ organisations notes: - Organisations on Github grew by 40% last year alone
## Open Source Benefits notes: - Larger community, higher quality
## GitHub Benefits notes: - Market Place with many integrations
## Responsibilities notes: - Maintainers need to response quickly otherwise people will lose interest - Living project - Marathon not a sprint
## Why not Open Source notes: - passwords / tokens / private keys / secrets are not a reason
## Conclusion notes: - Open source is like going to the gym, to get the benefit you need to put in the effort - You dont need faster horse, you need a car
